Delivery, Setup & Collection

These guidelines help ensure timely setup and smooth coordination with venues.

Setup Timing

  • Setup timing is arranged in advance and must be confirmed at least 2 weeks prior to the event date.
  • Customers should provide clear venue details (address, parking, loading bay, access instructions) before setup day.
  • Delays caused by venue access restrictions, waiting time, or unavailability of contact person may incur additional charges.

Arch Packages (Setup & Dismantling)

  • Arch packages include professional setup and dismantling unless stated otherwise.
  • Dismantling time is scheduled in advance and may be subject to venue operating hours and access.

Number Stack (Pre-Setup & Collection)

  • Pre-setup may be completed in advance, subject to schedule availability.
  • Collection will be arranged at a later agreed time after the event.
  • Please ensure a responsible person is available during collection to provide access and handover.
