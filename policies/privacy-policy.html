Privacy Policy

This policy explains how BalloonBash collects and uses personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details (e.g. name, phone number, email) and event details you submit via our enquiry form.
  • Any photos or references you provide for design inspiration (if applicable).

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries, provide quotations, and coordinate bookings.
  • To deliver our services (setup, delivery, collection coordination).
  • To improve our service and customer experience.

Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell personal information to third parties.
  • Information may be shared only when required to fulfill the service (e.g. coordination with venue staff when necessary).

Data Storage & Contact

  • Enquiries may be stored in our systems (including third-party form providers) for operational and record-keeping purposes.
  • If you would like to request access or removal of your personal data, please contact us via our contact page.
