Damage & Liability

Balloons are delicate by nature. The guidelines below clarify handling and responsibility.

Balloon Handling & Durability

  • Balloon décor is affected by heat, humidity, sharp objects, friction, and handling.
  • Colour tone and balloon sizes may vary slightly depending on availability and batch.
  • Minor balloon popping or deflation can occur and is considered normal wear for balloon products.

Responsibility After Setup

  • Once setup is completed and handed over, the customer/venue is responsible for the décor.
  • BalloonBash is not liable for damage caused by guests, children, pets, venue staff, or relocation of items.

Venue & Property

  • Customers are responsible for obtaining venue permission for setup and ensuring safe placement.
  • BalloonBash is not liable for venue restrictions, access issues, or any third-party claims arising from venue rules.
