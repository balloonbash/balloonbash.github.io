Cancellation & Refund Policy

Please review this policy before confirming your booking.

Deposits

  • Deposits are non-refundable once a booking is confirmed.
  • Deposits secure your event date/time and reserve resources for preparation.

Cancellations

  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before the event date will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
  • Event-day cancellations or no-shows will result in full forfeiture of all payments made.

Rescheduling

  • Reschedule requests must be made at least 7 days before the event date and are subject to availability.
  • Each confirmed booking is eligible for one reschedule.
  • Additional charges may apply if the new date requires changes in scope, logistics, or timing.
